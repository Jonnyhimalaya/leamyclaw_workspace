## leamymaths.ie — Detailed Findings

### Citability Breakdown
- **Best block:** "Privacy Overview" scored 58/100 (C) — ironically, the cookie policy is the most AI-citable content
- **"Packed with Valuable Resources":** 33/100 (F) — Stephen's bio, but too short and not self-contained
- **Course listings:** 27/100 (F) — just product cards, zero educational content on the page

### llms.txt Analysis
- **Exists** at `/llms.txt` — auto-generated by All in One SEO v4.9.5.1
- **1,517 links** across 14 sections (Posts, Pages, Products, Courses, Lessons, Quizzes, Modules)
- **Missing:** proper title format (`# Leamy Maths` exists but no `>` description)
- **No `/llms-full.txt`** — AI crawlers can't get detailed content summaries
- **Issue:** The sheer volume (1,500+ URLs) without descriptions means AI models get a sitemap but zero context about what each page teaches

### Critical Problems
1. **No educational content on public pages** — all value is behind WooCommerce login
2. **No FAQ blocks** — "What is Leaving Cert Higher Level Maths?" type content that AIs would cite
3. **No statistics/data** — pass rates, student outcomes, unique methodology claims
4. **No schema markup** for courses (EducationalOrganization, Course, FAQ structured data)
5. **llms.txt has zero descriptions** on 1,500+ links — AIs see URLs but don't know what they're about

### Recommendations (Priority Order)
1. **Add FAQ schema + content blocks** to homepage and course pages — target AI citation
2. **Create `/llms-full.txt`** with 2-3 sentence descriptions per key page
3. **Add educational content pages** (free, public) — "Guide to LC Higher Level Maths" etc.
4. **Add statistics:** "500+ students taught," "95% satisfaction rate," etc.
5. **Course schema markup** — `@type: Course` with syllabus, duration, price, instructor

## kilmurrylodge.com — Detailed Findings

### Citability Breakdown
- **Only 1 block detected** — "Gift Vouchers" paragraph scored 55/100 (C)
- The homepage is almost entirely images/visual with minimal crawlable text
- AI models have almost nothing to cite

### Critical Problems
1. **No llms.txt** — AI crawlers have no structured guide to the site
2. **Near-zero crawlable text** — the site is image-heavy with minimal HTML text
3. **No "About" content** on homepage — no mention of "4-star family-owned hotel" etc.
4. **No FAQ content** — "Is parking free at Kilmurry Lodge?" type questions
5. **No LocalBusiness schema** — missing structured data for Google AI Overviews
6. **No review/testimonial blocks** — massive missed opportunity for AI citations

### Recommendations (Priority Order)
1. **Create `/llms.txt`** — describe hotel, rooms, location, amenities, events
2. **Add text content blocks** to homepage — hotel description, location, amenities
3. **FAQ section** — parking, check-in, distance from city, wedding capacity, etc.
4. **LocalBusiness + Hotel schema** — structured data for AI discovery
5. **Review snippets** — surface TripAdvisor/Google reviews as structured data
6. **AEO monitoring** — track how AI models describe Kilmurry vs competitors
