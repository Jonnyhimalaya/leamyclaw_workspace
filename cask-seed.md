# CASK — Venue Intelligence Brief

## What is CASK?
CASK is a 600-capacity live music and events venue located at Kilmurry Lodge Hotel, Castletroy, Limerick, Ireland. It is a newly launched venue with no existing online presence, no social media accounts, and zero brand awareness in the market as of April 2026.

## The Venue
- Capacity: 600 (standing/seated configurations)
- Location: Castletroy, suburban Limerick — 5km from city centre, close to University of Limerick
- Parent property: Kilmurry Lodge Hotel (109 rooms, established hotel)
- Facilities: Full bar, stage, AV/lighting rig, green room, loading access
- Target events: Live music concerts, club nights, comedy, corporate events, private hire, tribute acts

## The Market
- Limerick city population: ~100,000. Greater Limerick ~200,000
- University of Limerick: 17,000+ students on doorstep
- Existing competitors: Dolans Warehouse (600 cap), UCH at UL (1,000 cap), Dolan's Pub (250 cap), various hotel ballrooms
- Limerick has a strong live music culture but limited mid-size venue supply
- Corporate events market: active — pharma, tech, and manufacturing companies in Limerick

## Current State (April 2026)
- No website for CASK specifically
- No Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or Twitter/X presence
- Not listed on any ticketing platforms (Ticketmaster, Eventbrite, etc.)
- No press coverage
- No bookings confirmed publicly
- Hotel guests and locals are largely unaware of the venue
- Internal hotel team aware but no external communications made

## Key Stakeholders
- Local music fans (18-35, UL students, young professionals)
- Event promoters (local and national touring promoters)
- Corporate event planners (Limerick business community)
- Hotel guests at Kilmurry Lodge
- Local media (Limerick Leader, Live 95, Limerick Post)
- National touring agents and booking agencies

## The Question
How does awareness and sentiment build across these groups in the first 90 days after a soft launch announcement? What are the critical inflection points and what actions drive the fastest credibility and awareness?
