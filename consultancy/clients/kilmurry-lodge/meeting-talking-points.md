**Date:** March 28, 2026

"I built an AI operations system for my own business. It runs 24/7, handles marketing, site management, analytics, and competitive intelligence — all coordinated by a central AI orchestrator. I want to build you the same thing, tailored for hotel operations."

1. Show Telegram — "This is how I talk to my orchestrator. I just message it like a person."
2. Show a recent exchange — membership management, analytics, anything concrete
3. Show Mission Control on your phone browser (localhost:3333 won't work — maybe screenshot it beforehand?)

## Key Messages

### 1. "This isn't a chatbot"
- It's a team of AI specialists, each with a defined role
- They have memory — they remember past decisions, learn from mistakes
- The orchestrator coordinates everything and talks to you in plain English

### 2. "Start small, prove value, then expand"
- Phase 1: Guest comms + review management (2 weeks)
- Phase 2: Operations coordination (month 1-2)
- Phase 3: Revenue management + marketing (month 2+)
- They'll see results in week 1

### 3. "Your data stays yours"
- Own server, completely isolated
- No sharing with any other business or client
- You control what the AI can and can't do (approval gates)

### 4. "This replaces tasks, not people"
- Front desk still runs check-in — but the AI drafted the welcome email already
- Housekeeping still cleans rooms — but the AI generated today's task list from the PMS
- GM still makes decisions — but the AI gathered the data and options

### 5. "I'm local"
- Based in Limerick, can walk in for support
- Not a SaaS product disappearing behind a support ticket
- Building a long-term relationship, not selling a licence

## Questions HE might ask

**"How much?"**
→ Infrastructure: ~€100-170/month (servers + AI APIs). My fee: [decide your rate — setup fee + monthly retainer]. The infrastructure cost is transparent and at-cost.

**"Is it secure?"**
→ Own dedicated server, data never leaves it, no third-party access, encrypted connections. More secure than most cloud hotel software.

**"What if it makes a mistake?"**
→ Human-in-the-loop. Nothing goes to a guest without approval until you trust it. Then you gradually give it more autonomy. Same way you'd onboard a new staff member.

**"How long to set up?"**
→ Basic system (guest comms + reviews): 1-2 weeks. Full operations: 4-6 weeks. Marketing/revenue: month 2-3.

**"Do my staff need to be tech-savvy?"**
→ No. They talk to it in plain English via WhatsApp or Telegram. If they can send a text message, they can use it.

## What to find out from him
- Their biggest daily time-wasters
- Their current tech stack (PMS especially — this determines what's possible)
- Budget comfort zone
- Who else needs to approve this
- Timeline — when does he want to start?
