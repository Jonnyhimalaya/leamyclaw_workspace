# Kilmurry Lodge Hotel — Discovery Notes

- **Location:** Castletroy, Limerick (adjacent to UL & National Technology Park)
- **Size:** 109 rooms
- **Type:** Family-run, business-focused hotel
- **Setting:** 14 acres of landscaped gardens
- **Proximity:** 5 min walk to UL, 10 min to Limerick city centre, 20 min Shannon Airport
- **Website:** kilmurrylodge.com
- **Contact:** GM is Jonny's friend

## Discovery Questions (for today's meeting)

### Operations — What hurts?
- [ ] What's your current booking system? (likely a PMS — Fidelio, Opera, Hotsoft, etc.)
- [ ] How do you handle OTA management? (Booking.com, Expedia, TripAdvisor — channel manager?)
- [ ] What's the front desk workflow? Check-in/check-out pain points?
- [ ] How is housekeeping coordinated? (paper lists, app, radio?)
- [ ] Maintenance requests — how are they logged and tracked?
- [ ] How many staff? Departments? (front desk, housekeeping, F&B, events, maintenance)

### Revenue & Marketing
- [ ] Who handles pricing/revenue management? Dynamic pricing or fixed rates?
- [ ] Do they run Google Ads, Meta ads? Who manages?
- [ ] How do they manage reviews? (TripAdvisor, Google, Booking.com responses)
- [ ] Email marketing to past guests? CRM system?
- [ ] Events/conferences — how are enquiries handled?

### Guest Communication
- [ ] Pre-arrival emails/texts? Automated or manual?
- [ ] How do guests contact the hotel? (phone, email, WhatsApp, website chat?)
- [ ] Post-stay follow-up? Review solicitation?
- [ ] Complaint handling process?

### Technology
- [ ] Current tech stack? (PMS, channel manager, POS, accounting)
- [ ] Website platform? (WordPress, custom, etc.)
- [ ] WiFi/IT infrastructure?
- [ ] Staff comfort level with technology?
- [ ] Any existing automation? (even basic stuff like auto-reply emails)

### Budget & Expectations
- [ ] What's the budget range they're thinking?
- [ ] Timeline expectations?
- [ ] Who's the decision maker? (GM alone or does ownership need to approve?)
- [ ] Are they open to phased rollout? (start small, prove value, expand)

## Key Conversation Points

### What to emphasise:
1. **You built your own system first** — this isn't theoretical, you're running it live for Leamy Maths
2. **Phased approach** — don't try to automate everything day one. Pick 2-3 high-impact, low-risk areas first
3. **Human-in-the-loop** — agents assist and automate, but staff stay in control. This isn't replacing anyone, it's giving them superpowers
4. **Data stays private** — their own server, their own data, completely isolated from any other client
5. **You're local** — in Limerick, can walk in and support them. That's huge vs some SaaS vendor

### What NOT to promise yet:
- Direct PMS integration (depends on their system's API availability)
- Anything touching payment processing
- Fully autonomous operation — start supervised, earn trust
