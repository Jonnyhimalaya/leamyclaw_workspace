AI Operations & Executive Support Discovery Questionnaire

Prepared for: Aidan

This is a short discovery questionnaire to help design your system around how your business actually runs, where the friction is, and where an AI chief-of-staff style setup could create the most leverage.

Answer as briefly or as fully as you like.

1. Business and Offers

1. How would you describe what you do in one or two sentences?
2. What are your main offers right now?
3. Which offer is the main revenue driver?
4. Who are your ideal clients?
5. What would a great next 12 months look like for you?

2. Workload and Bottlenecks

6. What takes up most of your time each week?
7. What parts of the business drain your energy most?
8. Where do things tend to get delayed, dropped, or forgotten?
9. What do you most want off your plate?
10. What should only ever be handled by you personally?

3. Sales and Client Journey

11. How do new clients usually find you?
12. What happens from first enquiry through to booking or engagement?
13. How are leads currently tracked and followed up?
14. Where is the biggest leak or inefficiency in the sales process?
15. If we improved one part of your pipeline first, what would make the biggest difference?

4. Content, Speaking, and Brand

16. How important is your personal brand to growth?
17. Which channels matter most right now?
18. What content are you currently creating, or want to create more consistently?
19. Do you already have talks, notes, recordings, or transcripts that could be repurposed?
20. What usually stops content from being published consistently?

5. Operations and Support

21. What admin, coordination, prep, scheduling, or follow-up work would you love to offload?
22. Do you have an assistant, ops person, or marketing support already?
23. Are there repeatable workflows in your business that happen over and over?
24. Who else, if anyone, would the system need to support besides you?

6. Systems and Tools

25. What tools do you currently rely on most?
26. Where does important business information currently live?
27. Which systems absolutely need to stay?
28. Which tools or workflows feel messy, manual, or underused?

7. Working Style and Priorities

29. How do you like support to communicate with you?
30. Do you want the system to mainly execute, or also challenge your thinking and spot blind spots?
31. When should it ask first, and when should it take initiative?
32. What would make you trust it quickly?
33. What would make it feel like a bad fit?

8. First Wins

34. If we could solve one problem in the first 30 days, what should it be?
35. What would make you say after one month, this is already worth it?
36. What are the top 3 areas where you want the system to create leverage first?

Final Question

If this system was working brilliantly for you 90 days from now, what would be noticeably different in your business and your day-to-day life?
