# Aidan — Business Snapshot

## Positioning
Aidan is a premium strategic advisor, high-performance specialist, and international keynote speaker. His work sits at the intersection of business growth, leadership performance, psychology, and execution. The business is built around the Back To Me ecosystem, including BTM, BTMB, live events, and a future book-led authority engine.

## Revenue Model
Primary revenue comes from founder and executive advisory retainers.
Secondary revenue comes from corporate half-day sessions, corporate full-day sessions, and private 10-hour advisory blocks.
Additional scale potential sits in BTM Personal Group Coaching, BTMB Business Mentorship, and live events.

## Ideal Clients
Founders, senior executives, leadership teams, and commercially responsible decision-makers who want measurable growth in revenue, performance, communication, clarity, and leadership.

## 12-Month Goal
Target revenue of €200k to €250k per month, driven by predictable inbound demand, stronger conversion, growing leveraged programs, a successful BTM book launch, and minimal time spent on operational admin.

## Core Constraints
- Lead generation is inconsistent
- Follow-up is manual and fragmented
- Content distribution lacks structure
- Testimonials and proof are underused
- Systems are fragmented across WhatsApp, email, notes, documents, Circle, Zoom, and memory
- Online authority does not yet match real-world reputation

## Strategic Diagnosis
This business does not mainly have a sales closing problem. It has a systemisation, authority distribution, and operational leverage problem. Once qualified conversations happen, close rates are strong. The main opportunity is to build a system that reliably turns expertise, delivery, recordings, and reputation into inbound demand and cleaner operations.

## Best First Use Cases
1. Lead-generation and positioning system
2. Sales pipeline and follow-up system
3. Content production and distribution system
4. Testimonial and proof extraction workflow
5. Executive assistant and coordination layer
6. Centralised business memory and knowledge layer
