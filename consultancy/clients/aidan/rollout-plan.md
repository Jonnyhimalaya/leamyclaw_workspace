## Phase 4 — Authority and Growth Engine

## Phase 5 — Dashboard and Delegation
- Add Mission Control if needed
- Support Aidan plus VA/ops support through structured workflows

## First 30-Day Pilot
Primary pilot: lead-generation and authority system.

Deliverables:
- clear positioning map
- pipeline visibility
- structured follow-up system
- repurposing workflow for existing recorded material
- consistent content and CTA rhythm

## Success Metrics
- 10 to 15 qualified inbound enquiries
- 5 to 8 booked calls
- 1 to 2 new clients closed
- daily content publication rhythm established
- newsletter reactivated
- pipeline visible and actively managed
