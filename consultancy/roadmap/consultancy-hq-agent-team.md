# Consultancy HQ Agent Team (Proposed)
**Purpose:** Separate the consultancy business from Leamy Maths over time.

---

## Why this exists
The consultancy will need its own:
- memory
- research bank
- playbooks
- proposal workflows
- client discovery support
- security/cost/design standards

This should eventually become its own OpenClaw workspace and team.

---

## Migration Principle
Until the consultancy is active enough to justify a dedicated workspace, keep building under `consultancy/`.

Once:
- client count grows
- research volume grows
- consultancy tasks become daily

...spin up the dedicated consultancy workspace and agent team.
