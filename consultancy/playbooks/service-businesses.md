# Service Businesses Playbook
**Purpose:** Reusable playbook for local service businesses, agencies, clinics, trades, salons, and other appointment/enquiry-driven SMEs.

---

## Why service businesses are a strong fit
They usually suffer from:
- missed follow-up
- enquiry overload
- owner/operator bottlenecks
- inconsistent quoting / replying
- review neglect
- fragmented communication across phone/email/WhatsApp

This makes them ideal for focused agentic systems.

---

## Common Pain Points
- slow response to leads
- missed calls not followed up
- quotes not chased
- appointments not confirmed cleanly
- reviews not requested/responded to
- customer information scattered across channels
- staff forget recurring admin tasks

---

## Best Early Wins
### 1. Lead / Enquiry Triage Agent
- classifies enquiries
- drafts replies
- routes to the right person
- flags urgent/high-value leads

### 2. Follow-Up Agent
- reminds owner/staff about open leads
- drafts follow-ups
- chases quotes / missed responses

### 3. Review / Reputation Agent
- monitors new reviews
- drafts replies
- prompts review requests after completed jobs

### 4. Daily Owner Briefing Agent
- open leads
- overdue follow-ups
- today's appointments/jobs
- outstanding issues
- reviews and urgent messages

### 5. Marketing / Content Agent
- local social content
- promotions
- seasonal campaign ideas
- proof/testimonial content

---

## Suggested Agent Team
- Orchestrator
- Lead/Enquiry Agent
- Follow-Up Agent
- Review Agent
- Daily Briefing Agent
- Marketing Agent

---

## Approval Model
### Safe early automation
- internal summaries
- reminders
- draft replies
- review monitoring

### Approve-then-act
- sending customer messages
- publishing promotions
- quote follow-up messaging

### Avoid early
- invoicing/payment adjustments
- legal/service commitments
- appointment changes with financial impact

---

## Discovery Questions
- where do leads come from?
- who replies and how fast?
- what happens after a missed call?
- how are quotes handled?
- what gets forgotten when things get busy?
- what systems are used (CRM, booking app, email, phone, WhatsApp, spreadsheets)?

---

## Strong Pilot Candidates
- lead triage + follow-up
- daily owner briefing
- review monitor
- missed-call recovery workflow
