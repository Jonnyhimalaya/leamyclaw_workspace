# Instagram Audience Targeting Recommendations

## Geographic Targeting (Both Campaigns)
- Location: **Limerick city + 30km radius**
- Include nearby commuter towns and suburbs
- People living in or recently in this location

---

## Campaign A: Parents Targeting

## Demographics
- Age: **35–55** (test split: 35–44 and 45–55)
- Gender: All (optional slight female skew test if budget allows)
- Language: English

## Interests/Behaviours (Meta detailed targeting)
- Parenting teenagers
- Secondary school education
- Leaving Cert / CAO / Irish education interests
- Education planning, tutoring, grinds
- Academic achievement / exam preparation

## Custom Audience Ideas
- Website visitors to parents page (30–90 days)
- Instagram engagers (365 days)
- Video viewers (if reel content already exists)

## Lookalike Suggestions
- 1% lookalike from highest-intent website visitors
- 1–3% lookalike from lead/contact submit list (if available)

## Messaging Priorities
- “Protect their CAO potential”
- “25 bonus points opportunity”
- “Limited spots, starts Monday”
- “Trusted local expert in Limerick”

---

## Campaign B: Students Targeting

## Demographics
- Age: **16–19** (core), optional expansion to 20
- Gender: All
- Language: English

## Interests/Behaviours
- Leaving Cert, study tips, exam prep
- Maths education, STEM interests
- Irish student life, school exam support
- Study motivation / productivity content

## Custom Audience Ideas
- Instagram engagers with student-focused content
- Viewers of student reels from this campaign
- Website visitors to student landing page

## Lookalike Suggestions
- 1% lookalike from student page visitors
- 1–3% lookalike from prior student leads/enquiries

## Messaging Priorities
- “Less stress, more confidence”
- “Last chance before exams”
- “Focused revision in key topics”
- “Starts Monday in Limerick”

---

## Placement Recommendations
Use Advantage+ placements but ensure creative-safe formatting for:
- Instagram Stories
- Instagram Reels
- Instagram Feed
- Instagram Explore (secondary)

If budget is tight, prioritize:
1. Stories
2. Reels
3. Feed

---

## Optimization & Testing Plan (Fast 5-Day Window)

## Day 1–2
- Launch 2–3 creatives per audience
- Track CTR, CPC, landing page views

## Day 3
- Pause low performers
- Duplicate best-performing copy into top placements

## Day 4–5
- Switch optimization toward conversions/leads
- Increase budget on urgency creatives (starts tomorrow/final spots)
- Add phone-first CTA in all final assets: **085 846 6670**

---

## Exclusions (to reduce waste)
- Exclude people outside Limerick radius
- Exclude converted leads where possible
- Exclude very young ages (<16)

---

## KPI Targets (Short Window Benchmarks)
- CTR link: aim > 1.2%
- CPC: keep as low as possible while maintaining local relevance
- Frequency: monitor for fatigue (>2.5 in 48h may need creative swap)
- Lead indicators: form submits, click-to-call taps, DMs
