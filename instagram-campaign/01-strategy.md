# Leamy Maths Instagram Ad Strategy (5-Day Sprint)

## Campaign Objective
Fill remaining spots for **Leaving Cert Higher Level Maths Easter Revision Courses** starting **Sunday, March 22, 2026** in Limerick.

Primary conversion goals:
1. Website lead/contact form submissions
2. Phone calls to **085 846 6670**

Secondary goal:
- DM inquiries from local parents/students

---

## Campaign Structure (Separate Audiences)

### Campaign A: Parents (Decision Makers)
- **Core message:** “Secure your child’s 25 bonus CAO points with expert support before it’s too late.”
- **Tone:** Reassuring, authoritative, urgent
- **Best formats:** Feed posts, Carousels, Stories with clear CTA, testimonial creative
- **Landing page:** https://leamymaths.ie/revision-for-leaving-cert-2026/

### Campaign B: Students (Influencers/Decision Partners)
- **Core message:** “Get exam-ready in 5 days with focused revision that boosts confidence and points.”
- **Tone:** Motivating, relatable, stress-relief + urgency
- **Best formats:** Reels, Stories, punchy Feed graphics, topic-based Carousel
- **Landing page:** https://leamymaths.ie/expert-revision-course/

---

## Creative Mix (Per Audience)
Use all key placements: **Stories (1080x1920), Reels (1080x1920), Feed/Carousel (1080x1080).**

- **Stories:** Daily urgency + countdown + swipe/click CTA
- **Reels:** Fast-paced, confidence-building, tutor-led credibility
- **Feed Static:** Clear offer + start date + location + phone
- **Carousel:** Topic breakdown + outcomes + social proof

---

## 5-Day Posting & Ad Activation Schedule

> Dates assume run-up from **Wed Mar 18 → Sun Mar 22**, with course start Sunday Mar 22.

## Day 1 (Wed Mar 18) — Awareness + Urgency Launch
**Parents**
- Feed static: “5 Days Left – Secure 25 Bonus CAO Points”
- Story: “Limited spots in Limerick” with call now/contact now

**Students**
- Reel: “Feeling behind in Maths? Here’s your rescue plan.”
- Story: “Start Monday. Last chance to revise properly.”

**Ad objective:** Traffic/Leads

## Day 2 (Thu Mar 19) — Topic Authority
**Parents**
- Carousel: Topics covered + why intensive revision works

**Students**
- Carousel/Reel: “What we cover in 5 days” (Financial Maths, Trig, Integration, etc.)

**Story for both:** “Which topic stresses you most?” (poll sticker for engagement in organic)

## Day 3 (Fri Mar 20) — Social Proof + Trust
**Parents**
- Feed/Testimonial creative: parent/student success feedback

**Students**
- Reel/Testimonial overlay: “I finally understood [topic] and felt exam-ready.”

**Story for both:** Tutor credibility + “Expert Leaving Cert prep in Limerick”

## Day 4 (Sat Mar 21) — Objection Handling + Offer Clarity
**Parents**
- Feed: “Worried it’s too late? It’s not.” + course structure + support

**Students**
- Story/Reel: “Can 5 days really help? Yes, if focused.”

**Both:** Clear mention of bundles and contact options

## Day 5 (Sun Mar 22) — Final Countdown Push
**Parents**
- Story sequence (3 frames): “Starts tomorrow” → “Limited spots” → “Call 085 846 6670”
- Feed static final call

**Students**
- Reel + Story: “Tomorrow we start. Don’t walk into the exam unprepared.”

**Ad objective:** Conversion / Leads with highest urgency budget

---

## Daily Story Cadence (Recommended)
- **Morning (8:00–10:00):** Reminder + countdown
- **Afternoon (13:00–15:00):** Topic spotlight / testimonial
- **Evening (19:00–21:30):** Strong CTA to landing page + phone number

---

## Budget Split (Suggested)
If budget is limited, prioritize conversion-focused ads:
- **60% Parents campaign** (likely stronger purchasing power)
- **40% Students campaign**

Within each campaign:
- 50% Stories/Reels
- 30% Feed static
- 20% Carousel

On final 48 hours (Sat/Sun): shift +20% spend into top-performing urgency creatives.

---

## Messaging Framework (Use across all creatives)
Every creative should include at least 3 of these:
1. **Urgency:** “Starts Sunday, March 22” / “Only a few places left”
2. **Outcome:** “Secure 25 bonus CAO points”
3. **Local relevance:** “In-person in Limerick”
4. **Trust:** “Expert tutor Stephen (Leamy Maths)”
5. **Action:** “Book now”, “Contact us”, “Call 085 846 6670”

## Tracking Recommendations
- Use separate ad sets for Parents vs Students with unique UTM links.
- Track:
  - Landing page views
  - Form submits
  - Click-to-call taps
  - DM inquiries
- Pause low-CTR creatives quickly (after 1,500–2,000 impressions if weak).
- Duplicate top-performing Story ad into Reel placement on Day 4/5.
